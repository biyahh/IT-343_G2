About Us

Welcome to One EVSU @118: Building Futures Through SMART Innovation. A celebration of Eastern Visayas State University’s 118th year of excellence in education, research, and community engagement. Join us as we move forward, building a future where knowledge meets innovation, and excellence leads to impact.
